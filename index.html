Our Vision & Mission

To renovate and re-construct and upkeep maintenance of ancient heritage places and institutions and to develop and maintain the same. 

To promote, protect, and preserve the rich cultural, mythological and historical heritage of India and pass it on to the coming generations.


To encouraging the fields of education having Indian Heritage value.

To inculcate the Sanathana Dharma and to help the poor and needy in the society – as service to human beings is service to God. 

About the Trust

Sarvalaya Samrakshana Trust has been created with the primary object of creating awareness and appreciation about ancient, unique and lesser known Indian heritage places and the rich cultural, mythological and spiritual evidence they carry. We strongly believe that behavioural change towards protecting and maintaining these great heritage treasures would happen once the history of these Indian heritage places is revealed. “Sarvalaya Samrakshana” (which in Sanskrit and tamil means ‘patronage, preservation, protection, Safeguard, conservation and maintenance of all kind Indian heritage places beyond the religion’) was founded in 23rd June, 2017 is a registered Public Charitable Trust and based in Chennai.
